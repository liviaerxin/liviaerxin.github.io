OpenCV tips

Q: Whether the image/frame from VideoCapture is in BGR or YUV pixels format? A: VideoCapture will convert the image automatically to BGR colorspace. you can disable this conversion (and receive YUV) by setting the CAP_PROP_CONVERT_RGB property to false.
